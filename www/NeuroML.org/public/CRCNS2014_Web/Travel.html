Travel Information


Sky Harbor Phoenix International Airport (PHX) is located about three miles from downtown Tempe and the location of the 2014 CRCNS PI Meeting. Voted Americaâ€™s Friendliest Airport, Sky Harbor is a major US airport with direct flights to most large cities in the US.

The hotel venue for the meeting offers daily airport transportation from 5:30am to 10:00pm. When you arrive at baggage claim and have your luggage, call the hotel at (480) 894-1400 to be directed to the curb-side location for the shuttle pick-up.

Due to the very short distance, catching a taxi from the airport to the hotel is an alternative and economical option.

The Phoenix METRO Light Rail System also offers convenient access to downtown Tempe from the airport. If you arrive at Terminal 4, you can walk directly to the PHX Sky Train, which will take you quickly to the METRO light rail station. From the other terminals, you must take the rental car shuttle to Terminal 4 and then get on the Sky Train. Once at the light rail station, purchase a ticket at the kiosk, and there are signs to let you know which direction to go toward Tempe and Mesa. You can exit at the Mill Avenue/Third Street Light Rail Station for a very short walk to the hotel.
