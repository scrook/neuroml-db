Restaurant Information


Downtown Tempe, known as the Mill Avenue District, has more restaurants concentrated in one area than any other restaurant row in the Valley. Dine inside or outside, from upscale and romantic to hole-in-the-wall specialties.

For a full listing and locations, see http://millavenue.com/directory/dining.
